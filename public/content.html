Project Overview

Animation Details
Timing: 30s
Format: 1920x1080p
Client: Microsoft
Deadline: January 15, 2015
Brief

An animation that communicates how Microsoft Cloud is the best method to help scale their teams. The project is to have an aesthetic that is modern, colorful with a combination of analog and digital aesthetics.

Script

Connected World

In today’s connected world, work does not have to be confined to the office. Being productive is having the ability to share and collaborate on information with virtually anywhere in the world.

So how can you take your great local team and turn it into a world-class team?

By foregoing the limitations of the office and allowing team members to contribute regardless of their location. Microsoft Cloud allows employees to work from virtually anywhere, the office, home or even on the move.

Cloud computing is here and Microsoft has both the knowledge and capabilities to provide you and your company with the necesary tools to thrive in today’s world.

Microsoft Cloud is here.
